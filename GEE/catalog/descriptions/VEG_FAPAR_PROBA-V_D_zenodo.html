Fraction of Absorbed Photosynthetically Active Radiation (FAPAR) at 250 m monthly for period 2014-2017 based on COPERNICUS land products

Long-term monthly Fraction of Absorbed Photosynthetically Active Radiation (FAPAR) median value at 250 m based on the time-series of COPERNICUS FAPAR . Derived using the data.table package and quantile function in R. Processing steps are available here . Antartica is not included.
