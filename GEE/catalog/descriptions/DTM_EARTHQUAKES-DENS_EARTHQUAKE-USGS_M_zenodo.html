Nighttime Lights PC1-4 based on the Version 4 DMSP-OLS Nighttime Lights Time Series 1997–2014. Derived using SAGA GIS Principal Component analysis. Image and data processing by NOAA's National Geophysical Data Center. DMSP data collected by US Air Force Weather Agency.
