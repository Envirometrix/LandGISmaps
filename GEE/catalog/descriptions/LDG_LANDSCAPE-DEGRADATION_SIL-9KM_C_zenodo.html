Map of 1992-2015 landscape change trajectories

Map of 1992-2015 landscape change trajectories. Landscapes are colored depending on their change trajectories and a percentage of changed area; small < 10%, medium (10% to 30%), and large (> 30%).

To access and visualize the map use: https://landgis.opengeohub.org/#/?base=OpenTopoMap&opacity=80&layer=ldg_landscape.degradation_sil.9km_c

Creation of this map is explained in details at https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0303243418305841 (preprint at https://eartharxiv.org/k3rmn/ ).
